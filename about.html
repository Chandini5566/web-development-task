About Central Library

The library has 63,155 volumes with 12,305 titles of books and about 3,525 back volumes of scientific and technical periodicals. The library also subscribes to nearly 115 National print journals and 41 magazines and more than 6,800 e-journals & 31,35,000 e-books. A separate reference section is maintained with Handbooks and General knowledge books. Interlibrary loan facility is available through DELNET. Central Library supports departmental libraries in all departments. The library follows an open access system encouraging the user to browse freely in the stock area.
